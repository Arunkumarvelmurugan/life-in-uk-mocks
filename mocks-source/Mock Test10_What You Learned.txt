🧠 What You Learned

These are the key facts and topics reinforced in this mock test. Review them to strengthen your knowledge before taking another mock test.


* 🌹 **St George's Day** → Celebrated on **23 April**, honouring the patron saint of England
* 🏞 **Lake District** → The **largest National Park in England**, closely associated with William Wordsworth
* 👑 **Boudicca** → Queen of the Iceni who led a major rebellion against the **Romans**
* 📖 **The Canterbury Tales** → Geoffrey Chaucer's famous collection of stories told by pilgrims travelling to Canterbury
* 🏦 **Bank Holidays** → The official name for public holidays in the UK
* 🌳 **Charles II** → Escaped capture after the Battle of Worcester by **hiding in an oak tree**
* 👑 **King Alfred the Great** → United the Anglo-Saxon kingdoms and defeated the **Vikings**
* 🖨 **William Caxton** → Introduced the **printing press** to England and became its first printer
* 🏰 **Beefeaters** → Yeoman Warders who guard and guide visitors around the **Tower of London**
* 🏔 **Bonnie Prince Charlie** → Supported by clansmen from the **Scottish Highlands** during the Jacobite Rising
* ⛪ **Elizabeth I** → Protestant monarch who restored the Protestant Church of England
* 🎬 **Charlie Chaplin** → Legendary silent film actor famous for **The Tramp**
* ✈️ **Battle of Britain (1940)** → The **Royal Air Force (RAF)** defended Britain against the German Luftwaffe
* 🗳 **Voting Rights** → The minimum voting age for UK general elections is **18**
* 🌼 **William Wordsworth** → Romantic poet inspired by nature and the beauty of the Lake District
* ⚔ **Battle of Waterloo (1815)** → Britain and its allies defeated **France** under Napoleon
* 👨‍💼 **William Wilberforce** → MP who led the campaign to abolish the **slave trade**
* ⛪ **Archbishop of Canterbury** → Spiritual leader of the **Church of England**
* 👑 **James I** → Previously **James VI of Scotland** before becoming King of England
* 🛒 **Shopping in the UK** → Most shops open **seven days a week**, though Sunday trading hours are often shorter
* 🚢 **Spanish Armada (1588)** → Fleet sent by **Spain**, defeated by England during Elizabeth I's reign
* 🏇 **National Horseracing Museum** → Located in **Newmarket, Suffolk**, the home of British horse racing
* 🏰 **Crathes Castle** → Historic castle located in **Scotland**
* 🏺 **Clarice Cliff** → Celebrated British designer renowned for her colourful **Art Deco ceramics**
