Q1. When does Easter take place?

❌ November or December

❌ January or February

✅ March or April

❌ May or June

Explanation: Easter takes place in March or April.

🧠 Memory Tip

Know why each option is correct or incorrect:

❌ November or December → These months include Remembrance Sunday, Christmas, and Boxing Day, but not Easter.
❌ January or February → These months include Burns Night (25 January) and sometimes the beginning of Lent, but Easter does not occur then.
✅ March or April → Correct. Easter always falls in March or April.
❌ May or June → These months contain Early May Bank Holiday and other public holidays, but not Easter.

Connect with the Christian calendar:

⏳ Lent → 40 days before Easter
✝ Good Friday → Death of Jesus
🌅 Easter Sunday → Resurrection of Jesus
🎉 Easter Monday → Public holiday
