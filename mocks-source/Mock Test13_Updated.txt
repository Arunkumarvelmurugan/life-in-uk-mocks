Q1. As a British citizen what are your responsibilities (choose two answers)?

❌ To walk your dog only during the day

✅ To respect and obey the law

❌ To go to church on Sunday

✅ To look after yourself and your family

Explanation: If you wish to be a permanent resident or citizen of the UK, you should: respect and obey the law, look after yourself and your family, respect the rights of others, treat others with fairness and look after the area in which you live and the environment.

🧠 Memory Tip

This is a SELECT TWO question.

Know why each option is right or wrong:

❌ To walk your dog only during the day → There is no UK law requiring dogs to be walked only during daylight hours. The legal requirement is that dogs wear a collar showing the owner's name and address in public.
✅ To respect and obey the law → One of the key responsibilities of every British citizen and permanent resident.
❌ To go to church on Sunday → The UK has freedom of religion. Nobody is required to attend church or follow any religion.
✅ To look after yourself and your family → Another important responsibility expected of citizens and permanent residents.

Remember the main responsibilities from the handbook:

⚖ Respect and obey the law
👨‍👩‍👧 Look after yourself and your family
🤝 Respect the rights of others
🌳 Look after your local area and the environment
⚖ Treat others fairly
