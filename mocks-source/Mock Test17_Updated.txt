Q1. World War II started as a result of Germany invading which country?

❌ France

❌ Russia

✅ Poland

❌ Austria

Explanation: The Second World War started when Hitler invaded Poland in 1939. Britain and France declared war in order to stop his aggression.

🧠 Memory Tip

Know why each country is incorrect or correct:

❌ France → Germany invaded France in 1940, after the Second World War had already begun.
❌ Russia → Germany invaded the Soviet Union in 1941 (Operation Barbarossa), not at the start of the war.
✅ Poland → Germany invaded Poland on 1 September 1939, starting the Second World War.
❌ Austria → Germany annexed Austria in 1938 (Anschluss), before WWII officially began.

Build a WWII timeline:

🇦🇹 1938 → Austria annexed
🇵🇱 1939 → Poland invaded → WWII begins
🇫🇷 1940 → France invaded
❄ 1941 → Soviet Union invaded
