Q1. Which of the following is a Crown Dependency but not part of the UK?

❌ Shetland

❌ Isle of Wight

✅ Isle of Man

❌ Anglesey

Explanation: The Channel Islands and the Isle of Man are closely linked with the UK but are not part of it. They have their own governments and are called 'Crown Dependencies'.

🧠 Memory Tip

Know what each place is:

❌ Shetland → A group of islands that are part of Scotland, and therefore part of the UK.
❌ Isle of Wight → An island off the south coast of England and part of the UK.
✅ Isle of Man → Correct. A Crown Dependency. It has its own parliament (Tynwald) and government but is not part of the UK.
❌ Anglesey → An island in Wales, therefore part of the UK.

Don't confuse these terms:

Isle of Man

Guernsey
🌍 British Overseas Territories

Neither group is part of the UK.
