Q1. Which of the following is a fundamental principle of British life?

❌ Communism

❌ Intolerance

✅ Individual liberty

❌ Inequity

Explanation: Individual liberty is the correct answer. The fundamental principles of British life include democracy, the rule of law, individual liberty, and tolerance.

🧠 Memory Tip

Know what each option means:

❌ Communism → A political and economic ideology. It is not one of the fundamental principles of British life.
❌ Intolerance → The opposite of a British value. British society promotes tolerance of people with different faiths and beliefs.
✅ Individual liberty → One of the four fundamental principles of British life.
❌ Inequity → Means unfairness or inequality. Britain promotes fairness, equality before the law and equal opportunities.

Learn the four principles together:

🗳 Democracy
⚖ Rule of Law
🕊 Individual Liberty
🤝 Mutual Respect & Tolerance
Quick Memory Rule

Think:

D-L-L-T

D = Democracy
L = Law
L = Liberty
T = Tolerance
