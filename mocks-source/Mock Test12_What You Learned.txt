🧠 What You Learned

These are the key facts and topics reinforced in this mock test. Review them to strengthen your knowledge before taking another mock test.


* 🌐 **United Nations (UN)** → Established in **1945 after the Second World War** to promote international peace and security
* 🪨 **Giant's Causeway** → UNESCO World Heritage Site located in **Northern Ireland**
* 👑 **Mary, Queen of Scots** → Executed in **1587** after being imprisoned by Queen Elizabeth I
* ⚔ **The Jutes** → One of the three Germanic tribes that settled Britain after the Romans left in **AD 410**
* 🕵 **James Bond** → One of the UK's highest-grossing and most famous film franchises
* 👨‍⚖️ **Youth Courts** → Cases are heard by **specially trained magistrates** or a **District Judge**
* 👑 **Charles II** → Returned from exile in the **Netherlands** to restore the monarchy in **1660**
* 🤝 **National Citizen Service (NCS)** → Open to **16- and 17-year-olds** to develop leadership and community skills
* 🧀 **Wallace & Gromit** → Nick Park's award-winning animated films that earned multiple **Academy Awards**
* 🧬 **Francis Crick** → Co-discovered the **structure of DNA** in 1953
* 🌍 **UK Diversity** → Around **10%** of people in the UK have a parent or grandparent born outside the UK
* 🛵 **Driving Laws** → The minimum age to ride a **moped** in the UK is **16**
* 🏛 **Tony Blair** → Introduced the **Scottish Parliament** and **Welsh Assembly** following devolution
* ⚖ **Small Claims Procedure** → Claims up to **£5,000** in Scotland and Northern Ireland
* 🏰 **White Tower** → Built by **William the Conqueror** as the centrepiece of the Tower of London
* 👨 **John Major** → Succeeded Margaret Thatcher and played a key role in the **Northern Ireland peace process**
* 🚨 **Reporting Terrorism** → Suspected terrorist or extremist activity should be reported to the **local police force**
* 🏊 **Ellie Simmonds** → Paralympic swimmer who won gold medals in **2008, 2012 and 2016**
* 🌹 **National Flower of England** → The **Rose**, also known as the Tudor Rose
* 🚂 **Isambard Kingdom Brunel** → Designed the **Great Western Railway**, one of Britain's greatest engineering achievements
* 🤝 **The Commonwealth** → Association of countries working together to promote **democracy and development**
* ⛪ **The Reformation** → Henry VIII's movement that broke from the authority of the Pope and established the **Church of England**
* 🍎 **Sir Isaac Newton** → Explained the **law of gravity** and the laws of motion
* 🎬 **Nick Park** → Academy Award-winning creator of **animated films**, especially **Wallace & Gromit**
