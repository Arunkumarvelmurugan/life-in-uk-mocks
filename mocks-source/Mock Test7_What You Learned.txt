🧠 What You Learned

These are the key facts and topics reinforced in this mock test. Review them to strengthen your knowledge before taking another mock test.


* 🗣 **Origins of the English Language** → Modern English developed from **Anglo-Saxon** and **Norman French** after the Norman Conquest
* 🇺🇸 **American Independence (1776)** → The 13 North American colonies declared independence over **"No taxation without representation"**
* 🏛 **The Commonwealth** → England became a republic after the execution of **Charles I** in 1649
* 🎬 **British Film Industry** → **Harry Potter** is one of the UK's most successful film franchises
* 🌾 **First Farmers in Britain** → Farming began around **6,000 years ago**
* 🌺 **First World War** → Ended at **11:00 am on 11 November 1918**, remembered each year on Armistice Day
* ⚖ **Forced Marriage Protection** → UK courts can issue legal protection orders to prevent forced marriages
* 🥇 **Mary Peters** → Won Olympic **gold** in the pentathlon at the **1972 Olympic Games**
* 👨‍⚖️ **Youth Courts** → Cases are heard by **up to three specially trained magistrates** or a **District Judge**
* 💻 **Alan Turing** → Invented the **Turing machine**, laying the foundations of modern computing
* 📖 **Domesday Book (1086)** → Records life in England after the **Norman Conquest**
* ⚛ **Ernest Rutherford** → Led the first team to **split the atom**
* 🎵 **Auld Lang Syne** → Traditionally sung at **New Year (Hogmanay)** celebrations
* ⚔ **English Civil War** → Fought between the **Cavaliers** (King) and the **Roundheads** (Parliament)
* 🏰 **Hadrian's Wall** → Home to the famous Roman forts **Vindolanda** and **Housesteads**
* ⛪ **Puritans** → Protestant reformers who favoured simple worship and supported Parliament during the Civil War
* ☘ **Fenians** → Irish nationalists who campaigned for complete independence from the UK
* 🎸 **Swinging Sixties** → The Beatles and The Rolling Stones became world-famous British pop groups
* 🏍 **Driving Laws** → The minimum age to drive a car or motorcycle in the UK is **17**
* 🏛 **House of Lords** → Peers are **appointed**, not elected, and review legislation
* ⛓ **Emancipation Act (1833)** → Abolished slavery throughout the British Empire
* 🚔 **Home Secretary** → Responsible for **crime, policing, immigration and national security**
* 🎞 **Wallace & Gromit** → Award-winning British **animated films** created by Nick Park
* ❤️ **Domestic Violence Laws** → Domestic abuse is a criminal offence, and offenders can be prosecuted regardless of gender or marital status
