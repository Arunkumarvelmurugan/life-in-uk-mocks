🧠 What You Learned

These are the key facts and topics reinforced in this mock test. Review them to strengthen your knowledge before taking another mock test.

* ☘ **St Patrick's Day** → Celebrated on **17 March**, honouring the patron saint of Northern Ireland
* 🏛 **Northern Ireland Assembly** → MLAs are elected using **proportional representation**
* 👨‍💼 **Sir Robert Walpole** → Recognised as Britain's **first Prime Minister**
* 🎭 **British Film Industry** → Emily Watson is a celebrated British actress who has been **Oscar-nominated but has not won an Academy Award**
* 💰 **Adam Smith** → Developed influential ideas about **economics** during the Enlightenment
* ⚓ **Admiral Nelson** → Commanded the British fleet and died at the **Battle of Trafalgar (1805)**
* 🎆 **Hogmanay** → Scotland's traditional **New Year's Eve** celebration on **31 December**
* 📖 **Hansard** → The official written record of debates in the UK Parliament
* 🏉 **Six Nations Championship** → The major annual international **rugby union** competition
* 👑 **House of Lords** → Life peers are appointed by the **Monarch** on the advice of the Prime Minister
* 🌏 **Captain James Cook** → Mapped the east coast of **Australia**
* 📰 **Crimean War** → The first war to be extensively covered by **newspapers and photographs**
* 🪨 **Stonehenge** → Prehistoric monument located in **Wiltshire**
* ⛪ **St Augustine** → The **first Archbishop of Canterbury**
* 🎰 **Gambling Laws** → The minimum age to enter betting shops and gambling clubs is **18**
* 🏛 **UK Government** → The United Kingdom is a **parliamentary democracy** and a constitutional monarchy
* 🍳 **Ulster Fry** → Traditional cooked breakfast of **Northern Ireland**
* 🏥 **Clement Attlee** → Elected Prime Minister in **1945**, introducing the NHS and major post-war reforms
* 🏅 **Olympic Games** → The UK has hosted the Summer Olympics **three times** (1908, 1948 and 2012)
* 🚢 **Industrial Revolution Canals** → Built to connect factories with towns, cities and ports for transporting goods
* 👨‍💼 **Civil Service** → Civil servants are recruited on **merit**, remain politically neutral and are not appointed by the government
* ✈ **Concorde** → Supersonic aircraft jointly developed by **Britain and France**
* 🚂 **Steam Power** → The key innovation that drove Britain's **Industrial Revolution**
* 👑 **Boudicca** → Queen of the Iceni tribe who led a major revolt against **Roman rule** in Britain
