🧠 What You Learned

These are the key facts and topics reinforced in this mock test. Review them to strengthen your knowledge before taking another mock test.

* ☘ **Shamrock** → National flower and symbol of **Northern Ireland**
* 🏆 **Battle of Waterloo (1815)** → The last major battle fought between Britain and France, where the Duke of Wellington defeated Napoleon
* 💷 **UK Banknotes** → Common Bank of England notes are **£5, £10, £20 and £50**
* 🏞 **Lake District National Park** → Located in **England** and home to Wastwater, Britain's favourite view
* 🇬🇧 **Responsibilities of British Citizens** → Respect others' rights, obey the law, care for your family and community
* 🎭 **William Shakespeare** → Born in **Stratford-upon-Avon**, England's greatest playwright
* 👩 **Equal Voting Rights (1928)** → Women gained the right to vote at age **21**, the same as men
* 🎆 **Hogmanay** → Scotland's traditional celebration of **New Year's Eve (31 December)**
* ☘ **Belfast** → Capital city of **Northern Ireland**
* 🇿🇦 **Boer War (1899–1902)** → Fought in **South Africa** between the British Empire and the Boer republics
* 🗳 **Electoral Registration** → Northern Ireland uses **Individual Electoral Registration**
* 🏛 **Reserved and Devolved Powers** → Defence and immigration are reserved to the UK Government, while health and education are devolved
* 🏛 **Standing for Public Office** → Most eligible UK, Irish and Commonwealth citizens can stand from the age of **18**
* 🌍 **Victorian Free Trade** → The repeal of the **Corn Laws (1846)** encouraged free trade and industrial growth
* ⚓ **Admiral Nelson** → Led the Royal Navy to victory at the **Battle of Trafalgar (1805)**
* 🌾 **Serfs** → Peasants who worked their lord's land during the **Middle Ages**
* 🚂 **Steam Power** → Drove the **Industrial Revolution** and Britain's rapid industrial growth
* 👤 **Members of Parliament (MPs)** → Can be contacted by letter, phone, constituency office or local surgery
* ⛪ **The Reformation** → Henry VIII broke with the Pope and established the Church of England
* 📖 **Domesday Book (1086)** → Commissioned by **William the Conqueror** to record land, people and property
* 📚 **Butler Act (1944)** → Introduced **free secondary education** in England and Wales
* ⚖ **Small Claims Procedure** → An affordable way to resolve minor **civil disputes**
* 🏛 **Crystal Palace** → Built for the **Great Exhibition of 1851** in Hyde Park
* 🏥 **Florence Nightingale** → Founder of modern nursing and known as the **"Lady with the Lamp"**
