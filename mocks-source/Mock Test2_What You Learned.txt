🧠 What You Learned

These are the key facts and topics reinforced in this mock test. Review them to strengthen your knowledge before taking another mock test.

* 🇬🇧 **Good Citizenship** → Respect and obey the law, care for your community and environment
* 🎃 **Halloween** → Celebrated on **31 October**
* 📜 **Bill of Rights (1689)** → Limited the Monarch's powers and strengthened Parliament
* 🏏 **The Ashes** → Famous cricket series between **England and Australia**
* 🏭 **Industrial Revolution** → Manufacturing became Britain's largest source of employment
* 🏴 **Battle of Bannockburn (1314)** → Robert the Bruce defeated the English, helping Scotland remain independent
* ⚖ **Criminal vs Civil Law** → Illegal smoking in enclosed public places is a criminal offence, while workplace and housing disputes are civil matters
* 🏴 **Massacre of Glencoe (1692)** → The MacDonalds of Glencoe were killed after failing to swear allegiance in time
* 🏛 **Devolved Governments** → Health and education are devolved powers, while defence and immigration are reserved to the UK Parliament
* 🌍 **First World War (1914–1918)** → Began after the assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand
* 🏴 **Edinburgh** → Capital city of Scotland and home to the Scottish Parliament
* ⛓ **Slavery Abolition Act (1833)** → Abolished slavery throughout most of the British Empire
* 🐑 **Highland Clearances** → Scottish crofts were cleared to make way for large sheep farms
* 🌍 **British Overseas Territories** → The Falkland Islands are linked to the UK but are **not part of the United Kingdom**
* ⚔ **English Civil War** → Key battles included **Marston Moor (1644)** and **Naseby (1645)**
* 👩 **Suffragettes** → Led by Emmeline Pankhurst, they campaigned for women's right to vote
* 🏞 **Lake District** → Home to Wastwater, voted Britain's favourite view in 2007
* 🪨 **Stone Age Britain** → The first people were **hunter-gatherers**
* ⛪ **The Reformation** → Henry VIII broke with the Pope and established the Church of England
* 💼 **National Insurance Number** → Apply through the **Department for Work and Pensions (DWP)**
* 🏇 **Royal Ascot** → One of Britain's most famous horse-racing events
* ⚖ **Scottish Jury System** → Criminal juries in Scotland have **15 members**
* ✝ **Lent** → The **40 days before Easter**
* 🏰 **Norman Conquest (1066)** → The last successful foreign invasion of England, led by William the Conqueror
