🧠 What You Learned

These are the key facts and topics reinforced in this mock test. Review them to strengthen your knowledge before taking another mock test.


* 🇬🇧 **Responsibilities of Citizens** → Respect and obey the law, look after yourself and your family, and contribute to your community
* 🪙 **Iron Age Britain** → The first coins minted in Britain were produced during the **Iron Age**
* 🏰 **Tower of London** → Built by **William the Conqueror** after the Norman Conquest
* 🏞 **National Parks** → Protected countryside where people can live, work and enjoy nature
* ⛪ **Established Churches** → England has the **Church of England**, while Wales and Northern Ireland have **no established church**
* 🏝 **Great Britain** → Consists of **England, Scotland and Wales**, excluding Northern Ireland
* 🏛 **Prime Minister's Questions (PMQs)** → Held **every week** while Parliament is sitting
* 👑 **Charles I** → Executed in **1649** after the English Civil War
* 🎭 **William Shakespeare** → Born in **Stratford-upon-Avon**, England
* ♿ **British Paralympians** → **Baroness Tanni Grey-Thompson** and **Ellie Simmonds** are among Britain's greatest Paralympic champions
* 🌎 **Elizabeth I** → Reigned when English settlers first began colonising the eastern coast of America
* 🗳 **Individual Electoral Registration** → Used in **Northern Ireland** for voter registration
* 🎄 **Christmas Day** → Celebrates the **birth of Jesus Christ** on **25 December**
* ✈️ **Battle of Britain (1940)** → A crucial **aerial battle** fought between the RAF and the German Luftwaffe
* 🐉 **Swansea** → One of the major cities of **Wales**
* 🤝 **2010 Coalition Government** → Formed by the **Conservative Party** and the **Liberal Democrats**
* ⛪ **Henry VIII** → Established the **Church of England** during the Reformation
* ☠ **The Black Death (1348)** → Plague that killed around **one-third** of the population in Britain
* 🥘 **Haggis** → Traditional national dish of **Scotland**, closely associated with Robert Burns
* ⚖ **Justice of the Peace Court** → Deals with **minor criminal offences in Scotland**
* 📖 **William I's Records** → The **Domesday Book** and the **Bayeux Tapestry** provide important information about his reign
* 🌍 **Irish Home Rule** → Delayed until **1921** because of the outbreak of the **First World War**
* 👸 **Elizabeth I** → Protestant queen who achieved a religious settlement balancing Catholics and Protestants
* 🆔 **National Insurance Number** → Requires proof of **identity** and **permission to work in the UK**
