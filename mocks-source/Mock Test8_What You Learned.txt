🧠 What You Learned

These are the key facts and topics reinforced in this mock test. Review them to strengthen your knowledge before taking another mock test.

* 🇬🇧 **Responsibilities of Citizens** → Look after yourself, your family, your local area and the environment
* 🔔 **Big Ben** → Located at the **Houses of Parliament** in London
* 🗳 **Equal Voting Rights (1928)** → Women gained the right to vote at **21**, the same age as men
* 🌍 **Geography of the UK** → The United Kingdom is located in **north-west Europe**
* 🌿 **St Andrew** → Patron Saint of **Scotland**
* 🌹 **Wars of the Roses** → Fought between the **House of York** and the **House of Lancaster**
* 📜 **Bill of Rights (1689)** → Strengthened Parliament and limited the powers of the monarch
* 🌺 **The Cenotaph** → Britain's national **war memorial** in Whitehall, London
* 👩 **Margaret Thatcher** → Britain's first female Prime Minister
* 🏛 **Speaker of the House of Commons** → Chairs debates and maintains order in Parliament
* ⚽ **Bobby Moore** → Captain of England's **1966 FIFA World Cup-winning** team
* 🚢 **Spanish Armada (1588)** → England defeated the Spanish fleet during the reign of Elizabeth I
* 👮 **Police Complaints** → Anyone can complain by writing to the **Chief Constable** or using the official complaints process
* ⚖ **Jury Service** → Available to eligible people aged **18–70** on the electoral register
* 🗳 **Jury Selection** → Jurors are chosen **randomly from the electoral register**
* 🏦 **Bank Holidays** → Public holidays when banks and many businesses close, though not everyone receives the day off
* 📬 **Voting Process** → A **poll card** is sent before an election, followed by a ballot paper at the polling station
* 👨‍⚖️ **Solicitors** → Usually charge clients based on the **time spent** working on a case
* 📺 **Impartial Broadcasting** → Television and radio must provide balanced political coverage during elections
* 🏝 **Crown Dependencies** → The **Channel Islands** and the **Isle of Man** are linked to the Crown but are **not part of the UK**
* ✈ **Sir Frank Whittle** → Invented the **jet engine** in the 1930s
* 🌼 **William Wordsworth** → Wrote **The Daffodils** and was inspired by the Lake District
* 🛡 **Oliver Cromwell** → Became **Lord Protector** after the English Civil War
* ✝ **Lent** → The **40-day Christian period before Easter**, traditionally observed through prayer, fasting and reflection
